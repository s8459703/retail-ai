Sales Dashboard

Interactive bar, pie, and line charts showing revenue by category (Beauty ₹8.26L · Clothing ₹9.39L · Electronics ₹9.33L) and monthly trends across 2023–2024.

ML Prediction

Linear regression model trained on 1,000 transactions predicts the next order value. Average transaction is ₹2,698 with a range of ₹100 – ₹7,960.

Multi-step forecasting predicts the next 10 transactions with a historical vs forecast chart, trend indicators, and summary stats (total, avg, high, low).

Dataset covers 1,000 customers aged 18–60 (avg 38.8), with near-equal gender split — 511 Female, 489 Male — across all product categories.
